Commerce After 10th

Commerce stream develops financial literacy, business understanding and management skills. It is suitable for students interested in accounting, entrepreneurship, finance, economics and corporate leadership.

Subjects in Commerce

Core Subjects

  • Accountancy: Financial statements, bookkeeping and auditing basics.
  • Business Studies: Business operations, management and organizational behavior.
  • Economics: Demand-supply, macroeconomics and Indian economy concepts.
  • Mathematics or Informatics Practices: Data handling and practical analytical skills.
  • English and Entrepreneurship: Communication, presentation and startup mindset.

Career Opportunities

Popular Careers

  • Chartered Accountant (CA), Company Secretary (CS), Cost Accountant (CMA).
  • Banking and Finance: Investment analyst, loan officer, financial planner.
  • Management and Business: HR manager, operations manager, entrepreneur.
  • Taxation and Audit: Tax consultant, auditor, compliance officer.
  • MBA pathways in finance, marketing, business analytics and international business.
