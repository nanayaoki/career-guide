Government (MPSC / UPSC)

Government careers offer public service, administration, policy-making, and leadership. They are suited for students aiming for civil services and stable long-term roles.

Introduction

What this field is

Government careers include civil services, state administration, defense services, railways, and public sector jobs. UPSC and MPSC are major routes for administrative leadership roles.

Study Requirements

Path after 10th

  • 12th can be from any stream, though arts, commerce, or science can all work well.
  • Graduation in BA, B.Com, B.Sc, or other recognized degree courses for UPSC/MPSC eligibility.
  • Strong general knowledge, communication, and current affairs study are important.
Exams Required

Entrance options

  • UPSC Civil Services Examination.
  • MPSC State Services Examination.
  • Other government exams such as SSC, banking, railways, and defense recruitment.
Career Opportunities

Popular jobs

  • IAS Officer
  • IPS Officer
  • State Civil Services Officer
  • Railway Officer
  • Government Administrator
