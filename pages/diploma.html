Diploma After 10th

Diploma (Polytechnic) is a practical and job-oriented route for students who want technical skills early. It combines classroom theory with workshops, labs and industry exposure, leading to direct jobs or lateral entry to engineering.

Subjects in Diploma

Popular Diploma Branches

  • Mechanical Engineering: Design, manufacturing, thermodynamics and machine tools.
  • Electrical Engineering: Circuits, power systems, motors, control and wiring.
  • Civil Engineering: Surveying, construction materials, structural concepts and planning.
  • Computer Engineering: Programming, networking, hardware and software development.
  • Additional core topics: Applied mathematics, engineering graphics, communication and workshop practice.

Career Opportunities

Popular Careers

  • Junior Engineer roles in manufacturing, construction and utilities.
  • Site supervisor, CAD technician, maintenance engineer, quality inspector.
  • Computer support, network technician and software testing assistant roles.
  • Public sector opportunities in railways, PWD, electricity boards and PSUs.
  • Entrepreneurship: Small workshops, fabrication units, repair services.
