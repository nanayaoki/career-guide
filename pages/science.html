Science After 10th

Science stream is ideal for students interested in technology, medicine, research and innovation. It offers strong analytical foundations and opens both engineering and healthcare pathways through PCM and PCB choices.

Subjects in Science

Core Subjects

  • Physics: Laws of motion, energy, electricity, optics and modern physics.
  • Chemistry: Organic, inorganic and physical chemistry concepts.
  • Mathematics: Algebra, calculus, trigonometry and statistics.
  • Biology: Human body, genetics, botany, zoology and biotechnology.
  • Computer Science: Programming fundamentals, data structures and IT tools.
  • PCM Branch: Physics + Chemistry + Maths for engineering and technology careers.
  • PCB Branch: Physics + Chemistry + Biology for medical and life science careers.

Career Opportunities

Popular Careers

  • Engineering: Mechanical, Computer, Civil, Electrical, AI and Robotics.
  • Medical: Doctor, Dentist, Pharmacist, Physiotherapist, Nursing.
  • Technology: Software Developer, Data Analyst, Cybersecurity Specialist.
  • Research and Academia: Scientist, Lab Researcher, Professor.
  • Defense and Government: NDA, technical services, scientific organizations.
