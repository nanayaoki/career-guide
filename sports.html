Sports

Sports careers involve physical performance, coaching, fitness, sports science, and management. They suit students who want discipline, training, and competition-based growth.

Introduction

What this field is

The sports field includes athletics, team games, sports medicine, coaching, physical education, and event management. It offers both performance and support roles around games and fitness.

Study Requirements

Path after 10th

  • 12th can be from any stream depending on the course and institution.
  • Degrees like BPEd, B.Sc Sports Science, BBA Sports Management, or diploma courses in fitness.
  • Professional training, coaching certifications, and continuous practice are essential.
Exams Required

Entrance options

  • Sports trials and merit selection for academies and universities.
  • Fitness or coaching certifications for specialist roles.
  • Some institutes conduct entrance tests for sports science or physical education programs.
Career Opportunities

Popular jobs

  • Professional Athlete
  • Coach
  • Fitness Trainer
  • Sports Physiotherapist
  • Sports Manager
