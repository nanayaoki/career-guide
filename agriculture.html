Agriculture

Agriculture combines farming, soil science, agribusiness, food production, and technology to improve crop yield and rural development.

Introduction

What this field is

Agriculture is the science and business of growing crops, managing livestock, and using modern tools to improve food production. It includes agri-tech, research, and government support roles.

Study Requirements

Path after 10th

  • 12th Science stream is preferred for many agriculture degree programs.
  • Degrees like B.Sc Agriculture, B.Tech Agricultural Engineering, or related agri-science courses.
  • Students can also continue into M.Sc Agriculture or specialized agri-business studies.
Exams Required

Entrance options

  • ICAR entrance exams for agriculture-related admissions.
  • State-level agriculture university entrances.
  • CUET or institute-level merit tests for selected programs.
Career Opportunities

Popular jobs

  • Agricultural Officer
  • Farm Manager
  • Soil Analyst
  • Agribusiness Executive
  • Crop Scientist
