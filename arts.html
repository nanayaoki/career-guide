Arts After 10th

Arts stream focuses on society, culture, communication, human behavior and governance. It is excellent for students with interests in social sciences, media, legal studies, public administration and creative expression.

Subjects in Arts

Core Subjects

  • History: Civilizations, historical events and modern world development.
  • Geography: Physical geography, human geography and environmental studies.
  • Political Science: Constitution, governance systems and public policy.
  • Psychology: Human behavior, cognition, personality and counseling basics.
  • Sociology and Languages: Society, communication, literature and critical thinking.

Career Opportunities

Popular Careers

  • Journalism and Media: Reporter, editor, content strategist, anchor.
  • Law: Advocate, legal advisor, judiciary and legal research roles.
  • Civil Services and Government: IAS, IPS, administrative and policy roles.
  • Psychology and Social Work: Counselor, therapist, NGO professional.
  • Education and Humanities: Lecturer, researcher, language specialist.
