ITI After 10th

ITI (Industrial Training Institute) courses are designed for quick skill-based employment. Students get trade-specific practical training with apprenticeship opportunities and can start earning earlier in technical sectors.

Subjects in ITI

Popular ITI Trades

  • Electrician: Wiring, safety, motors, industrial electrical systems.
  • Welder: Arc welding, gas welding, fabrication and metal work.
  • Fitter: Assembly, machine fitting, measurement and maintenance.
  • Mechanic: Automobile systems, engines, diagnostics and servicing.
  • Other trades: COPA, plumber, diesel mechanic, refrigeration and AC technician.

Career Opportunities

Popular Careers

  • Electrician, welder, fitter, mechanic and machine operator positions.
  • Jobs in manufacturing plants, railways, construction and service centers.
  • Apprentice to permanent technician route in government and private sectors.
  • Self-employment: Repair shop, welding unit, electrical contracting.
  • Further education through diploma bridge courses and specialized certifications.
